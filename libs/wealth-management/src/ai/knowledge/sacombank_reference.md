# Sacombank Profit & Rewards Guide

## 1. Sacombank Visa Platinum Cashback

Objective: Maximize savings on online and foreign transactions.

- **5% Cashback on Online Payments**:
  - Covers all domestic & international online channels.
  - **MCC Note**: Excludes Utilities (4900), Securities (6211), and Government/Tax (9311, 9399).
- **3% Cashback on Foreign POS**:
  - Direct spending at terminals outside Vietnam.
- **Card Fees**:
  - **Annual Fee**: 599,000 VND.
  - **Foreign Transaction Fee**: 3.75% (Best for spending above 5M VND to net-profit after cashback).
  - **Cash Withdrawal**: 2% (Min 20k).
- **Travel Protection Alpha**: Global travel insurance up to **11.6 billion VND**.

## 2. Sacombank Visa UNIQ Platinum

Objective: 20-50% savings on daily logistics (Supermarkets & Transport).

- **The UNIQ 50/20 Strategy**:
  - **50% Cashback**: First transaction within first 30 days.
  - **20% Cashback**: Supermarket & Transport categories.
- **MCC Identification (20% Categories)**:
  - **Supermarket/Convenience**: 5411, 5499, 5722, 9751 (Winmart, Co.op Mart, Bách Hoá Xanh, 7-Eleven).
  - **Transport/Ride-hailing**: 4121, 5969, 4789 (Grab, Be, Xanh SM, Vexere).
- **Card Fees**:
  - **Annual Fee**: 599,000 VND.
  - **Debit/Credit Hybrid**: Use as Debit at domestic ATMs for **Free Withdrawals**.
- **Profit Cap**:
  - Max cashback: **600,000 VND / billing cycle**.
  - Strategy: Allocate exactly 1.5M VND of Supermarket/Commute spend to hit the 300k category cap first.

## 3. General "Alpha" Exclusions

- **No Rewards for**: MCC 6211 (Securities), 7800-7802 (Casinos), 5944 (Jewelry/Gold), 4900 (Utility Bills).
- **Interest-Free Window**: 55 days.
