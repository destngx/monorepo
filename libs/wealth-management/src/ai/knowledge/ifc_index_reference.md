# IFC (Investment Fund Certificate) Profit Guide

## 1. Fmarket Platform Operations

Objective: Strategic execution for lower fees and optimized entry/exit.

- **Execution Timelines (The NAV Cycle)**:
  - **Buy/Sell Orders**: Can be placed 24/7, but only match on the fund's **Matching Day** (Ngày khớp lệnh).
  - **Matching Day**: Usually daily (D) or selected days (e.g., Tue/Thu). Orders must be confirmed and funds transferred before the **Cut-off Time** (usually 10:30 AM or 14:30 PM on D-1 or D).
  - **Settlement**:
    - **Buy**: Units appear in 1-2 days after Matching Day.
    - **Sell**: Cash reaches bank account in 2-5 business days after Matching Day.
- **Conversion Strategy (The Swap)**:
  - You can **Convert** (Chuyển đổi) between funds with the same manager (e.g., DCDS -> DCBF) on Fmarket.
  - **Alpha**: Conversion fees are often lower than Sell + Buy fee cycles. Efficient for rotating from Equity to Bonds during market downturns.

## 2. Yield & Fee Optimization

- **Zero-Fee Entry**: High-tier funds like **DCDS, DCBC, DCBF** often have **0% Buy Fees** on Fmarket.
- **Holding Period Alpha**:
  - Sell fees are tiered. Always check the **Exit Fee Schedule**:
    - < 1 year: ~1.0% - 2.0%
    - 1-2 years: ~0.5% - 1.0%
    - > 2 years: **0% (Pure Profit)**.
  - Strategy: Only invest capital that can be sidelined for 12+ months to avoid high exit friction.
- **SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)**:
  - Use Fmarket's SIP to automate monthly buys. This achieves **Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)**, smoothing out the high volatility of the VN-Index.

## 3. Best Practices for Profit

- **Market Timing**: Entry is best when the **VN-Index P/E** is below historical averages (e.g., < 12x).
- **Platform Security**: Fmarket is licensed by the SSC. Funds are held by supervisory banks (e.g., BIDV, Standard Chartered), not Fmarket itself.
- **Diversification**: Split allocation between **Growth (Equity)** funds for bull markets and **Defensive (Bond/iSAVING)** for preservation.

## 5. Fund Manager Rules & Best Practices

### VinaCapital (VMEEF/VESAF/VEOF)

- **Trading Day**: Every business day (T).
- **Strategy**: Very active management. Best for catching "Modern Economy" growth sectors.
- **Tactical Tip**: Redemption money usually arrives fast (T+2 to T+3).

### VCBF (VCB-AIF/MGF/BCF)

- **Trading Day**: Business days (AIF/MGF) or Tue/Thu (some others).
- **Rule**: Minimum holding period of **24 months** is critical to avoid high 2-3% exit fees.
- **Tactical Tip**: Automatic 0.1% PIT (Personal Income Tax) deduction on sell orders.

### SSIAM (SSISCA/VLGF)

- **Trading Day**: Daily (T).
- **Rule**: Recent fee update (May 2025) increased short-term redemption friction (2.5%).
- **Tactical Tip**: **Switching** between SSISCA and SSIBF (Bonds) is often **Free**, allowing for zero-cost tactical asset allocation.
