# Details: Narrative Synthesis - The Final Confirmation

## 1. Feature Meaning: The Market Story

**Narrative Synthesis** (via News Analyze) confirms if the macro indicators (Gold/VNIndex) match the market's "Story."

## 2. API Source & Logic

- **Primary Source**: `POST /api/news/analyze` (topic: `Interest Rates Vietnam`).
- **Confirmation Tone**: If bias is `BEARISH` on a key macro topic, this confirms that the rotation from Stocks into Cash/Gold is not a "Flicker" but a "Trend."

## 3. Business Use Cases (Actionable)

- **Sentiment Shield**: Reduces the risk of "Falling Knife" scenarios (Buying a dip in a news-negative environment).
- **Thematic Rotation**: AI-driven "News Insight Cards" suggest rotating from "Banking" into "Export" sectors if the sentiment on VND devaluation is high.

## 4. Why This Hub?

This is the "Final Confirm" of the **Market Rotation Pipeline**. It interprets the raw numbers from `Market Pulse` and `Fmarket`.
