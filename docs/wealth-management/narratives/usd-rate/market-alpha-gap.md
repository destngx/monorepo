# Details: The Binance & Crypto "Market Alpha" Gap

## 1. Feature Meaning: The "Black Market" Signal

The **Market Alpha Gap** refers to the price difference between "Official" (Bank) and "Informal" (Binance P2P/Global Exchange) USD rates in Vietnam.

## 2. Competitive Edge: The Arbitrage Indicator

- **The Gap**: When Binance USD rates are $>2\%$ higher than Bank rates, it indicates a **"Flight from VND."**
- **Analytical Value**: This is a leading indicator for local market devaluations or increased risk-off sentiment in the Vietnamese stock market.

## 3. Business Use Cases (Actionable)

- **Arbitrage Detection**: Users holding USDT on Binance can see their "Surplus Value" relative to a bank withdrawal.
- **Inflation Warning**: Large spreads act as a warning to move from local bank savings into gold or USD-denominated assets.

## 4. Current State & Missing Pieces

The **Fmarket API** does NOT provide this crypto-rate. This signifies a need for a secondary **Binance Connector API** for high-frequency market analysis, which can be shared with the "Market Pulse Terminal."
