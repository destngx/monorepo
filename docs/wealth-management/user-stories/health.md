# User Stories: Financial Health (The Physical)

## 1. Persona Definition: Resilient Saver

- **Role**: Specialized Wealth Manager focused on safety nets, emergency fund coverage, debt management, and asset diversification.
- **Needs**: Holistic health score, diagnostic indicators (Ratios), benchmarking, and prescriptive "Doctor's Orders."

---

## 2. Diagnostics & Ratios

### US-HEA-001: Holistic Financial Health Score (0-100)

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want to see a single "Overall Health Score" represented as a circular gauge, so that I can have a baseline for my financial robustness.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Score must be weighted: 30% Savings, 30% Emergency Fund, 20% Debt, 20% Diversification.
  - Gauge must use color-coding (Red: < 50, Yellow: 50-75, Green: > 75).

### US-HEA-002: Institutional-Grade Expense Coverage Gap

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want to see my "Emergency Fund" expressed as "Months of Expenses Covered" (e.g., 6.5 months), so that I know exactly how long I could survive a job loss.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Calculation: (Total Cash Liquidity / Average Monthly Expenses).
  - Benchmark target MUST be 6 months by default.

---

## 3. Benchmarking & Analysis

### US-HEA-003: Debt-to-Income (DTI) Snapshot

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want to see my DTI ratio compared to the 35% safe threshold, so that I don't over-leverage my future income.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Ratio must pull from "Debt/Liability" accounts vs. "Income" transactions.
  - Must display a "Safety Status" (e.g., "Over-leveraged" vs. "Healthy").

### US-HEA-004: Asset Diversification Radar Chart

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want a 5-point "Radar Chart" showing my score across Cash, Stocks, Bonds, Real Estate, and Crypto, so that I can visually identify where my portfolio is "Out of Balance."
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Points on the radar must be based on a 0-10 score for each asset class concentration level.

---

## 4. Prescriptive Action (AI)

### US-HEA-005: AI-Powered "Doctor's Orders"

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want a 3-point "Prescription" list for how to improve my health score, so that I have a clear roadmap for my next financial moves.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - AI must analyze my highest-weighted vulnerabilities (e.g., "Low Liquidity").
  - Each order must be actionable (e.g., "Increase savings by 5%").

---

## 5. Security & Verification

### US-HEA-006: "Show Your Math" Audit Trail

- **Story**: As a Resilient Saver, I want to click a ratio (e.g., 6.2 months) and see the exact formula and numbers used to calculate it, so that I can verify the system's accuracy with my own spreadsheet.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Popover must show: (Numerator [Cash]) / (Denominator [Avg Expenses]) = Result.
  - Must display the 6-month historical average for expenses.
